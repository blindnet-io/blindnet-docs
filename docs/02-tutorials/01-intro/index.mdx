# DevKit - Introductory Tutorial

The purpose of the **intro** tutorial is get you started quickly with blindnet devkit.

After completing this tutorial, you will be able to:
  - Expose a Privacy Portal where your users can make Privacy Requests
  - Configure your Application's privacy settings to reflect your data processing practices
  - Automate responses to the `TRANSPARENCY` requests

Going further, you can read additional sections to take advantage of the full functionality of the blindnet devkit.

Let's get started!
